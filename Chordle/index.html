Jazz Chordle

Inspired by the original chordle. You do not need to spell the chord correctly, each note just needs to be in the right octave and position.

  • Green means: that note is in the right spot and position.
  • Yellow means: that note is in the right spot but wrong octave.
  • Blue means: that is in the wrong spot but right octave.
  • Gray means: that note is nowhere in the chord.
